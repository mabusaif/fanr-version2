Our Story

The idea began in 2022 when daily challenges in the real estate development journey were recurring: multiple parties, delayed completion, lack of transparency, and weak digital integration.

From this challenge, and as a result of our long experience in the sector, we innovated FANR as a solution that gathers all parties on a single platform: the project owner, provider, contractor, and supervisor, within an integrated digital experience that reduces waste and increases efficiency.
